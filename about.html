Our Story

Founded with passion and creativity, PowerPuff Nail Salon has been transforming ordinary nails into extraordinary works of art since 2020.

What started as a small studio with three passionate nail artists has grown into Astana's premier destination for innovative nail design and luxury nail care.

We believe that beautiful nails are more than just aesthetics - they're a form of self-expression, confidence, and personal style.
